# 📚 Page Pay Reading Sheets

**Free printable reading tracker worksheets for kids — where every page earns a penny!**

A simple, fun way to motivate young readers. Kids track the pages they read each day and earn 1 penny per page. Great for summer reading, school year routines, or any time you want to build a reading habit.

---

## 📄 The Sheets

| Level | For | Description |
|-------|-----|-------------|
| **Level 1: Early Reader** | Grades K–1 (ages 5–7) | Single-day sheet with penny coloring circles, reading feelings, and book rating |
| **Level 2: Growing Reader** | Grades 2–3 (ages 7–9) | 7-day reading log with earnings tracker, money helper, and book reflection |

## 💡 How the Page Pay System Works

1. Child reads pages from any book
2. They fill in their daily reading sheet
3. For every page read → they earn **1 penny**
4. At the end of the session, count the pages and pay out the pennies!

> **Tip:** Use a clear jar so kids can *see* their pennies grow. It's incredibly motivating!
